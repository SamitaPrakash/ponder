02 — How it works
How it works
Three steps.
Zero friction.
Step 01
Hold & speak
Hold anywhere on the screen. Talk freely — no prompts, no structure. Up to 90 seconds. Smallest.ai transcribes your voice in real-time.
Step 02
Release
Claude instantly distills your stream of consciousness into 3 key thoughts, 1–2 clear actions, and a single word for your current tone.
Step 03
Review
Your thoughts, surfaced and organized. Check off actions, revisit your history, see patterns across the day.
